About Empire Records

In Empire Records, we follow a rag-tag group of misfits two nights and one full day in their lives as record store employees outside of Atlantic City, NJ. With an amazing alternative soundtrack of rock and grunge from the early 90s, this film has something for everyone whether you're the straight-A student trying to handle the pressure from home or just a lost shoplifter looking for their place to belong.

Company Credits: Regency Entertainment

Release Date: October 20, 1995

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Music, Romance

MPAA Rating: PG-13

Running Time: 1h 30min
